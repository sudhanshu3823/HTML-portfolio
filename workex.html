My Work Experiences!

My time at Ramoji Film City allowed me to gain valuable insights into retail management, merchandising strategies, and working in a fast-paced environment. It also enhanced my ability to work effectively under pressure and improved my interpersonal skills, which are essential in client-facing roles.
